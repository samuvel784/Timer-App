This Privacy Policy explains how the Timer Application ("the App") handles user data. Your privacy is a priority, and this application is designed with a privacy-first approach.

1. Overview

The App is a productivity tool that allows users to create and manage timers. It operates primarily offline and does not require account creation.

2. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, or location.

Local Data Storage: The following data may be stored locally on your device:

  • Timer durations and presets
  • Timer history
  • User preferences (theme, sound settings)

3. Permissions Used

  • Notifications: Required to alert users when timers complete.
  • Foreground Service: Ensures timers continue running accurately in the background.
  • Exact Alarm Permission: Used to maintain precise timing functionality.
  • Boot Completed (optional): Restores timers after device restart.

4. How Data Is Used

All data is used strictly within the app to provide timer functionality. No data is transmitted to external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

6. Third-Party Services

Currently, the app does not use third-party services such as analytics or advertisements.

Future Notice: If services like analytics or cloud sync are added, this policy will be updated accordingly.

7. Data Security

All data remains on your device. We implement standard practices to ensure data integrity and prevent unauthorized access.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. User Control

  • Users can delete all stored data by clearing app storage.
  • Users can disable notifications anytime.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
